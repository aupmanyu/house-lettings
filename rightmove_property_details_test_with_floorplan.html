Full description

A modern and well presented one bedroom apartment in the iconic development The Circle found within the heart of Shad Thames. The property benefits from a large reception room, separate kitchen, large bedroom with walk in wardrobe and good size balcony. Available fully furnished to a high standard and offers wooden floors throughout.
The Circle is a beautiful residential building which is located on the South Bank of the Thames and has a concierge office while further benefiting from being surrounded by a range of trendy shops and eateries of Shad Thames.
